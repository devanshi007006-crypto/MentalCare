⚠️ Immediate Crisis & Urgent Help

**If you or someone else is in immediate danger or actively attempting self-harm, please call your local emergency services (e.g., 911) right now.** Do not wait.

Crisis Support Helplines (24/7)

These services are free, confidential, and available around the clock for emotional distress or crisis intervention.

  • Global Directory: Use Find a Helpline for anonymous and confidential support resources (phone, text, or webchat) in **over 130 countries**.
  • India: Contact the Vandrevala Foundation for free 24x7 mental health counselling: +91 9999 666 555 (Call or WhatsApp).
  • India: Tele-Manas for mental health support: 14416.

Educational & Professional Guidance

Trusted resources for mental health information, advocacy, and finding long-term professional care.

  • Mental Health Education: HelpGuide.org offers free, evidence-based guides on managing anxiety, depression, stress, and more.
  • Advocacy & Support: The National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) provides support and education for individuals and families affected by mental illness.
  • Finding a Therapist: Look for licensed therapists or counselors through trusted directories in your country.
  • Local Care: Talk with a doctor or primary healthcare professional in your area to get referrals or check for local counseling services (e.g., college or workplace programs).

Links are for information only; verify services in your region. This content is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
